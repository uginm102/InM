Debit and Prepaid Card Management

Use our Internet and Mobile Banking self-service card management tools to securely manage your cards and perform common card-related actions directly from your device.

  • Step 1
    Log in to On The Go with mobile with your Fingerprint or Password.
  • Step 2
    Tap on Cards button
  • Step 3
    Select the Prepaid Cards option
  • Step 4
    Tap the 3 dots and select the Top-up prepaid Card option
  • Step 5
    Select from your accounts the one you wish to fund the card
  • Step 6
    Confirm whether it is your card or another that you wish to top-up. If your own, select the top-up currency
  • Step 7
    Enter the top-up amount and add transaction remarks
  • Step 8
    Mark the checkbox to confirm details above and tap Submit to continue
  • Step 9
    Enter generated one-time password (OTP) to complete the card top-up
