Debit and Prepaid Card Management

Use the On The Go mobile app's self-service card management tools to securely top up your Prepaid Card directly from your device in minutes.

1

Log in to On The Go

Open the On The Go mobile app and log in using your Fingerprint or Password.

2

Tap on Cards

From the home screen, tap the Cards button.

3

Select Prepaid Cards

Choose the Prepaid Cards option from the card list.

4

Open Top-Up option

Tap the 3 dots (⋮) menu and select Top-up Prepaid Card.

5

Select funding account

Choose the account you wish to fund the card from.

6

Confirm card & currency

Confirm whether it is your own card or another. If yours, select the top-up currency.

7

Enter amount & remarks

Enter the top-up amount and add any transaction remarks.

8

Confirm & Submit

Mark the checkbox to confirm your details, then tap Submit to continue.

9

Enter OTP

Enter the generated one-time password (OTP) to complete the card top-up.
